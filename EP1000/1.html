Week 1: Cardboard Phone Stand

We were supposed to create a phone stand out of cardboard. I first measured my phone, then sketched out what I wanted to do, which was two triangular slopes connected to one another.

After that, I found the cardboard I wanted to use and drew the slopes onto the blank side of the cardboard

My first prototype was small and could not hold up the phone. So I tried again. The second one would be big enough to hold my phone, however, it was a bit unstable. I added two cardboard pieces at the end to make it sturdier as seen below.
