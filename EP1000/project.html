Project Plan: The Grooveshroom

I am planning on creating a music reactive lamp that is shaped in a mushroom. When music is played or when someone is singing, the light in the mushroom will either dim or brighten depending on the volume of the sound.

Materials Needed

  • Button (1x)
  • Sound Detector/Microphone (1x)
  • NeoPixel LEDs strip (1-3x)
  • Wires (as many as you need)
  • Arduino Board (1x)
  • Breadboard (1x)
